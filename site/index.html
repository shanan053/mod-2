OUR MENU

Chicken

Made by marinating & frying chicken bites coated with flour, curry leaves and spices.Curry leaves & garlic are the key ingredients in this dish.

Beef

Beef Wellington is a pie made of fillet steak coated with pâté (often pâté de foie gras) and duxelles, which is then wrapped in parma ham and puff pastry, then baked.

Sushi

A staple rice dish of Japanese cuisine, consisting of cooked rice flavoured with vinegar and a variety of vegetable, egg, or raw seafood garnishes and served cold.
